Spongebob Squarepants

Spongebob Squarepants is a sea sponge who lives in Bikini Bottom. He is the main character of the show Spongebob Squarepants. He is an employee at the Krusty Krab. His best friend is Patrick Star along with his pal Sandy the Squirrel. He has a pet snail named Gary and a grumpy neighbor named Squidward. He enjoys catching jellyfish and playing the flute.
